# Import a Wallet

If you already have a wallet, you can import it to Buidler using your seed phrase or private key. This will enable you to use your existing wallet to access the Buidler platform.

To get started, download the Buidler app for [iOS](https://buidler.app/download/ios) or [macOS](https://buidler.app/download/mac). Then, follow these steps to import your wallet:

### For iOS

**1. Open the Buidler app and click on the "Import Wallet" button.**

<div class="mobile-screenshot">

![](../../static/img/using-buidler/import-wallet/import-wallet.png)

</div>

**2. Enter your seed phrase or private key**. Be sure to double-check the information you're entering to avoid making any mistakes.

<div class="mobile-screenshot">

![](../../static/img/using-buidler/import-wallet/import-seed-phrase.png)

</div>

**3. Create a password for login on Buidler**. Choose a strong and memorable password. This password will be used to encrypt and decrypt your seed phrase. Learn more about it in [this article](https://docs.buidler.app/docs/blog/how-buidler-store-your-password)
<div class="mobile-screenshot">

![](../../static/img/using-buidler/create-wallet/create-password.png)

</div>

**4. Congratulations,** your wallet will be imported and you are now a part of the Buidler community.

<div class="mobile-screenshot">

![](../../static/img/using-buidler/create-wallet/create-wallet-success.png)

</div>

### For macOS

**1. Open the Buidler app and click on the "Import Wallet" button.**

![](../../static/img/using-buidler/import-wallet/mac-import-wallet.png)
    
**2. Enter your seed phrase or private key**. Be sure to double-check the information you're entering to avoid making any mistakes.

![](../../static/img/using-buidler/import-wallet/mac-import-seedphrase.png)

**3. Create a password for login on Buidler**. Choose a strong and memorable password. This password will be used to encrypt and decrypt your seed phrase. Learn more about it in [this article](https://docs.buidler.app/docs/blog/how-buidler-store-your-password)

![](../../static/img/using-buidler/create-wallet/mac-create-password.png)


**4. Congratulations,** your wallet will be imported and you are now a part of the Buidler community.

![](../../static/img/using-buidler/create-wallet/mac-create-wallet-success.png)

That's all there is to it! With just a few simple steps, you can import your existing wallet onto the Buidler.
